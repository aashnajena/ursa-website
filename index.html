About Us

At UrsaGlow Lighting Industries Private Limited, we are redefining the future of energy-efficient LED lighting. Established in January 2025 and headquartered in Bhubaneswar, Odisha, we specialize in cutting-edge LED solutions that not only minimize energy consumption but also elevate user experiences.

From design and assembly (CKD/SKD) to OEM collaborations and full-scale manufacturing, we bring innovation to every stage of the process. Our product range spans Smart LED Lighting, Decorative and Architectural Lighting, Human-Centric Lighting (HCL), and Advanced Energy-Efficient Solutions—each crafted to set new industry benchmarks.

At the helm of UrsaGlow is our Managing Director, Manas Kumar Jena, a visionary with 36 years of expertise in Research & Development (R&D) and Engineering. Under his leadership, we proudly champion the "Make in India" initiative, fostering robust local manufacturing capabilities and driving technological advancements in the lighting industry.

At UrsaGlow, we don’t just create lights—we craft experiences, enhance spaces, and pave the way for a brighter, more sustainable future.
